### Task Learnings: Admin Analytics and Job Queue Integration

1. **Bull Queue Integration:**
   - Background jobs were previously stubs or direct async calls.
   - Integrated `bull` for reliable task processing and retry logic.
   - Centralized queue configuration in `src/config/queue.ts` allows for consistent job naming and Redis connection management.

2. **Real-time Analytics with Prisma:**
   - Replaced hardcoded projections with time-series data from Postgres.
   - User growth: Calculated as the average daily signup rate over the last 30 days.
   - Financial Run Rate: Projected monthly revenue based on month-to-date performance and 30-day trends.
   - Cohort Retention: Implemented a specific calculation for users who returned in Week 2 after having a session in Week 1.

3. **Data Sufficiency Guards:**
   - Implemented `insufficient_data` flags to prevent misleading statistics when sample sizes are small (e.g., < 7 days of growth data or < 10 qualifying users for retention).

4. **Performance Optimization:**
   - Added indexes on `users(created_at)` and `sessions(started_at)` to support fast analytics queries on large datasets.

5. **Error Handling:**
   - Implemented `NotFoundException` for missing resources (like jobs) to provide clearer API responses compared to generic 500 errors.
